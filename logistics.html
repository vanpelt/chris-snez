Lodging

HotWire is a reasonable resource for finding hotels in the city. The best area's to look for are Union Square East, SOMA, or Nob Hill. You'll want to steer clear of Civic Center.

I would also suggest checking out Air BnB. This search shows you people that are willing to rent their room out ordered by distance from my apartment. There is also a Hostel / Hotel a block from our apartment Hotel Elements.

If you are having trouble finding a place, I can reach out to some friends and probably find you something, just let me know.

Transportation

You can fly into San Francisco (SFO) or Oakland (OAK). SFO is a bit easier to get into the city via public transportation. BART will take you near my home "A" or downtown to SOMA / Union Square. The public bus and trolley system (MUNI) is also very convenient. You can easily get away without having a car in San Francisco
